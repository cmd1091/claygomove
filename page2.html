Clean as you go
movement

The goal of this website is to raise awareness to clean your surroundings after you litter.
You should learn to clean the place where you did your task.

Cleanliness can be made possible if all of us will be united for one goal which is the cleanliness of our surroundings. Cleanliness can make our surroundings more pleasant plus, it will make it safer for everyone because harmful diseases that is found from dirty objects will not be present.

We are encouraging everyone to clean their surroundings to help preserve our nature which is being destroyed by pollution which is usually trash. Having a dirty surrounding can also affect the health of people because disease carrying bacterias infect people who are exposed.

It will also lessen the work of our janitors who works hard for the cleanliness of our school.
